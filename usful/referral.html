Referral Form Guidance

This guidance explains how to refer someone for support through Reconnect Us. It is designed to help referrers provide the right information so we can respond clearly, safely, and appropriately.

Purpose of this form

This referral form is for individuals who would benefit from support with digital skills, access to online services, and building confidence with technology.

Reconnect Us supports people in a friendly, safe, and practical way, helping them become more confident and independent when using digital devices and online services.

Who can make a referral?

  • Support workers
  • Probation staff
  • Community organisations
  • Housing staff
  • Family members or carers
  • The individual themselves

Who is this support for?

  • People who need help using a mobile phone, tablet, or computer
  • People who need support setting up or using email
  • People who need help accessing online services such as NHS, DWP, Universal Credit, or council services
  • People who want help with video calling, messaging, and staying connected
  • People who need support building confidence with everyday digital tasks
  • People who would benefit from learning about online safety and awareness

Before completing this form

  • The person being referred knows about the referral
  • They have agreed to be referred
  • The contact details provided are correct
  • Enough information has been included for us to understand the support needed

Information to include

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address, if available
  • Preferred contact method
  • Brief summary of the support needed
  • Any relevant barriers or additional needs
  • Referrer name and organisation
  • Referrer contact details

Consent

Reconnect Us must have the person’s consent before support can begin.

Please confirm that the individual:

  • Understands what they are being referred for
  • Agrees to their information being shared with Reconnect Us for support purposes

What happens next?

  • The referral will be reviewed by Reconnect Us
  • We may contact the individual or the referrer for further information
  • Suitable support will be arranged based on need and availability
Safeguarding: If there is an immediate risk of harm or a serious safeguarding concern, do not use this form as the main response. Please contact the appropriate emergency service or safeguarding team straight away. Reconnect Us provides digital support and guidance, but does not replace emergency, medical, or specialist crisis services.
