RECONNECT-US Equal Opportunities & Diversity Policy

Purpose

This policy outlines RECONNECT-US’s commitment to treating everyone with dignity, fairness, and respect. It supports an environment free from discrimination, harassment, and victimisation, and promotes diversity, inclusion, and equal access to services and opportunities.

1. Scope

This policy applies to all staff, volunteers, trustees, clients, contractors, and anyone representing or working with RECONNECT-US in any capacity.

2. Commitment to Equality and Diversity

  • We value diversity and recognise that different backgrounds bring fresh ideas, skills, and perspectives
  • We are committed to providing an inclusive environment where everyone has equal opportunity to participate, contribute, and achieve their potential
  • We will actively challenge prejudice, discrimination, and inequality wherever it occurs

3. Legal Framework

This policy reflects our obligations under:

  • Equality Act 2010
  • Human Rights Act 1998
  • Other relevant UK equality and anti-discrimination legislation

We recognise all protected characteristics under the Equality Act 2010:

  • Age
  • Disability
  • Gender reassignment
  • Marriage and civil partnership
  • Pregnancy and maternity
  • Race, including colour, nationality, and ethnic or national origin
  • Religion or belief
  • Sex
  • Sexual orientation

4. Responsibilities

  • Management: Ensure this policy is implemented, monitored, and regularly reviewed
  • Staff and Volunteers: Uphold this policy in all actions and decisions, and treat colleagues, clients, and partners respectfully
  • Clients: Respect diversity and inclusion while using our services

5. Recruitment and Opportunities

  • All recruitment and selection decisions will be based on merit, skills, and ability
  • Job and volunteer adverts will be written inclusively and made widely accessible
  • Reasonable adjustments will be made to support applicants and participants with disabilities

6. Service Delivery

  • Services will be accessible to all, removing barriers wherever possible
  • Diverse cultural, language, and accessibility needs will be considered in service design and delivery

7. Unacceptable Behaviour

RECONNECT-US will not tolerate:

  • Discrimination, harassment, or bullying in any form
  • Victimisation of anyone who raises concerns about discrimination or unfair treatment

8. Complaints and Reporting

  • Anyone who feels they have been discriminated against or treated unfairly may raise a concern through the Complaints Policy
  • All reports will be investigated promptly, fairly, and confidentially

9. Monitoring and Review

  • Policies, practices, and outcomes will be reviewed regularly to ensure equality and diversity goals are met
  • This policy will be reviewed annually or when legislation or organisational needs change
