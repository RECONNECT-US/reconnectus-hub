Reconnect Us Chess Club on chess.com

Looking for something positive to do in your spare time? Reconnect Us has a chess club on chess.com, and it is a brilliant way to keep your mind active, meet new people, and have a game whenever you feel like it.

Join the club

Club name: Reconnect Us

You can play chess with people online from your own home, your sitting room, or wherever you are comfortable. They do not need to be in the same room as you. They could be in another town, another city, or the other side of the world, and you can still have a proper game because it is all online.

Play anytime you are bored

If you have spare time, feel bored, or just want something different to do, chess is there when you need it. It gives you something to focus on, helps pass the time in a good way, and can be a really enjoyable part of your day.

Challenge someone you know

You can message or ring somebody and say you fancy a game. If they are free, you can both go on chess.com and play each other straight away without needing to travel anywhere.

Meet people through chess

It is also a great way to get to know people. Online chess gives you a reason to connect, chat, and build confidence with others through something simple and enjoyable.

Easy, flexible and at your own pace

You can play casually, take your time, learn as you go, and enjoy it in a relaxed way. Whether you are new to chess or already like the game, there is space for you in the Reconnect Us club.

Fancy a game? Pick up the phone, message someone, or go online and see who is about. A game of chess can start from your own sofa and connect you to someone anywhere in the world.
Good for the mind. Good for connection. Good for passing the time well.
