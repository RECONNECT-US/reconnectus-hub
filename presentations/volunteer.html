Volunteer Induction

Volunteer Mandatory Training & Checks

Before commencing any duties with RECONNECT-US, all volunteers must complete the required training modules and pass the required check so that staff, learners, and volunteers are protected from the start.

What This Covers

  • Mandatory training all volunteers must complete
  • The required suitability check before starting
  • Minimum expectations before any duties begin

Core Aim

  • Keep RECONNECT-US safe and compliant
  • Support safer work with vulnerable individuals
  • Make sure volunteers start with the right knowledge

Required Training

Every volunteer must complete the following mandatory training before beginning any role with RECONNECT-US.

Training Modules 1–3

  • Prevent — understanding how to safeguard individuals from radicalisation
  • Introduction to Health & Safety — awareness of workplace safety responsibilities and procedures
  • Mental Health Awareness — recognising signs of poor mental health and supporting wellbeing

Training Modules 4–5

  • GDPR (Data Protection Essentials) — understanding and complying with data protection regulations
  • Safeguarding Adults — identifying and responding to concerns about vulnerable adults

Required Check

Alongside the training, each volunteer must also complete the required suitability check.

Mandatory Check

  • Basic DBS Check — to help confirm suitability for working with vulnerable individuals

Why It Matters

  • Supports safer recruitment and safer volunteering practice
  • Helps protect learners, staff, volunteers, and the organisation
Volunteers will not be permitted to begin work within RECONNECT-US until all training modules are completed and a satisfactory DBS check is confirmed.

Before Starting Duties

This checklist makes the expectation simple: no one starts volunteering until the essentials are done.

Must Be Completed

  • All mandatory training modules completed
  • Basic DBS check confirmed as satisfactory

Before Work Begins

  • Volunteer should understand safeguarding, data protection, and safety expectations
  • Volunteer is then cleared to begin duties with RECONNECT-US
