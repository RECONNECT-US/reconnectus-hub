Benefits & Financial Support

What this training module is for

This module helps you practise how to think about benefit questions and which information is needed, without entering any real personal details like address, phone number or National Insurance number.

How this module works

  • You stay inside this RECONNECT-US training app – you do not have to open a real benefits site.
  • Use the practice exercises below to choose answers for a made-up person called Alex.
  • Talk through why you picked each answer and what might change in a real claim.
  • When you are finished, press the green Save button so this module shows as completed for the current learner.
