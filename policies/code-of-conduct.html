RECONNECT-US Code of Conduct & Behaviour Policy

Purpose

This policy outlines the standards of behaviour expected from everyone involved with RECONNECT-US. It aims to create a safe, respectful, and professional environment where staff, volunteers, and clients can engage positively.

1. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, volunteers, clients, contractors, and partners involved with RECONNECT-US, whether onsite, online, or at any event related to the organisation.

2. General Principles

  • Treat everyone with respect, dignity, and fairness
  • Act professionally and honestly in all dealings
  • Promote a positive, inclusive, and supportive environment
  • Respect confidentiality and privacy
  • Take responsibility for actions and accept feedback

3. Expected Behaviour

  • Communicate politely and clearly
  • Avoid offensive language, bullying, or harassment
  • Be punctual, reliable, and committed
  • Follow all RECONNECT-US policies
  • Respect diversity in culture, background, and beliefs
  • Use technology responsibly and ethically
  • Report unsafe or inappropriate behaviour immediately

4. Unacceptable Behaviour

  • Discrimination, harassment, or bullying
  • Violence, threats, or intimidation
  • Breach of confidentiality
  • Substance misuse affecting performance
  • Theft, fraud, or misuse of resources
  • Behaviour damaging RECONNECT-US reputation

5. Consequences of Breach

  • All breaches will be investigated seriously
  • Disciplinary action may include warnings, suspension, or removal
  • Serious incidents may be reported to authorities
  • Clients may have access to services reviewed

6. Reporting Concerns

  • All individuals are responsible for reporting concerns
  • Reports can be made confidentially
  • Concerns should be raised with a Senior Manager or safeguarding lead
  • All reports will be handled fairly and promptly

7. Review

This policy will be reviewed annually or as required to ensure it remains effective and relevant.
