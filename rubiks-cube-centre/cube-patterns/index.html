How to Use This App

Pick a pattern, start with a solved cube, then press Next or Slow Play. The correct side of the cube turns as one complete layer.

  • R means right side clockwise.
  • R' means right side anti-clockwise.
  • R2 means right side twice.
  • Reverse to Solved turns the moves backwards.

You can drag the cube with your mouse or finger to view it from a better angle.
