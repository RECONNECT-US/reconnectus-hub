Training notes

  • Paying on time is one of the biggest habits for credit health.
  • Using too much available credit can make lenders see someone as stretched.
  • Lots of applications in a short time can look risky.
  • A poor score is not permanent. Small steady habits can rebuild confidence over time.
  • Buy Now Pay Later should still be treated like debt, even when it feels easy at checkout.
