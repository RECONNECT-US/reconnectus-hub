Benefits Maximiser Simulation Game

This is a simulation game inside the RECONNECT-US financial awareness programme. It does not check real entitlement, does not calculate real benefit amounts, and is not welfare rights advice.

The aim is to help learners practise spotting possible support routes and understand when to use official calculators or speak to an adviser.

Waiting

Suggested learner journey

  1. Gather accurate information: income, rent, savings, household, health, caring, and current benefits.
  2. Use an official benefits calculator for a real estimate.
  3. Speak to Citizens Advice, Turn2us, a local welfare adviser, housing officer, probation support worker, or trusted support service if unsure.
  4. Do not ignore letters, rent arrears, council tax, or benefit decisions. Ask for help early.
  5. Keep copies of forms, messages, decisions, fit notes, rent proof, and medical evidence where needed.
