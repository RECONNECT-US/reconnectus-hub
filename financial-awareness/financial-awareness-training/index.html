Why this matters

Debt often starts small. A £20 payment here, a £15 subscription there, a Buy Now Pay Later order somewhere else — then suddenly someone has several payments due before payday.

This platform helps learners slow down, understand the true cost, and recognise when borrowing is becoming pressure.
