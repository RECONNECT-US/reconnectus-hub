Volunteer Application Guidance

This guidance explains how to complete the volunteer application form for Reconnect Us. It is designed to help gather the right information so we can understand each applicant’s interest, suitability, availability, and support needs.

Purpose of this form

This volunteer application form is used to register people who would like to volunteer with Reconnect Us.

It helps us understand who the applicant is, what kind of volunteering they are interested in, and how their skills, experience, and availability may support our work.

Who should complete this form?

  • Anyone interested in volunteering with Reconnect Us
  • People applying for learner support, digital support, admin, outreach, or community-based roles
  • Applicants who would like to offer skills, time, or experience to support others

What should applicants include?

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address, if available
  • Preferred contact method
  • Areas of interest or preferred volunteer role
  • Relevant skills, experience, or interests
  • Days or times they may be available
  • Any support needs, accessibility needs, or important information we should be aware of

Before completing this form

  • Please make sure the details provided are accurate
  • Complete all key sections as fully as possible
  • Be honest about availability, confidence, and experience
  • Let us know if any adjustments or support may be needed during the application process

Suitability and safer recruitment

Reconnect Us wants volunteering to be positive, safe, and well-matched.

Some roles may require further discussion, references, training, or other checks depending on the nature of the role and who the volunteer will be supporting.

Consent

By completing the application, the applicant is confirming that the information provided is correct to the best of their knowledge.

  • The applicant understands they are applying to volunteer with Reconnect Us
  • They agree for their information to be used for volunteer recruitment and contact purposes
  • They understand that submitting a form does not automatically guarantee a volunteer position

What happens next?

  • The application will be reviewed by Reconnect Us
  • We may contact the applicant for more information or an informal discussion
  • If suitable, the applicant may be invited to the next stage of the volunteer process
  • Any role offered will depend on current opportunities, suitability, and availability
Important note: Reconnect Us is committed to creating a safe and respectful environment for volunteers and the people we support. Some volunteer roles may involve working with vulnerable individuals, so additional checks, boundaries, and safeguarding expectations may apply.
