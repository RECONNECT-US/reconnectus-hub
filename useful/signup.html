Learner Sign-Up Guidance

This guidance explains how to complete the learner sign-up form for Reconnect Us. It is designed to help gather the right information so support can begin clearly, safely, and in a way that suits the learner’s needs.

Purpose of this form

This learner sign-up form is used to register people who would like support from Reconnect Us.

It helps us understand who the learner is, what support they need, and how we can best help them build confidence with technology and online services.

Who should complete this form?

  • The learner themselves
  • A support worker helping the learner
  • A family member or carer with the learner’s agreement
  • A professional supporting the learner to access digital help

Who is this sign-up for?

  • People who want to improve their digital skills
  • People who need help using a phone, tablet, or computer
  • People who want support accessing online services
  • People who want help setting up email or using the internet
  • People who want to build confidence and independence with technology
  • People who may need extra support due to barriers such as literacy, confidence, or lack of experience

Before completing this form

  • Please make sure the learner understands why the form is being completed
  • Check that the learner agrees to sign up for support
  • Make sure the contact details provided are correct
  • Include enough information so we understand what kind of help is needed

Information to include

  • Learner full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address, if available
  • Preferred contact method
  • Brief description of what support the learner would like
  • Any barriers, accessibility needs, or additional support needs
  • Name of the person completing the form, if different from the learner

Consent

Reconnect Us must have the learner’s consent before support can begin.

Please make sure the learner:

  • Understands what they are signing up for
  • Agrees to share their details with Reconnect Us for support purposes
  • Knows they can ask questions before support begins

What happens next?

  • The sign-up form will be reviewed by Reconnect Us
  • We may contact the learner, or the person completing the form, for more information
  • Support will be arranged based on the learner’s needs and availability
Important note: If there is an immediate risk of harm or a serious safeguarding concern, this form should not be used as the main response. Please contact the appropriate emergency service or safeguarding team straight away. Reconnect Us provides digital support and guidance, but does not replace emergency, medical, or specialist crisis services.
